We Define Authentic Beautiful Spaces

Ambience Interiors is a team of dedicated, dynamic, passionate and creative interior designers whose main aim is to deliver ultimate customer satisfaction through implementing high quality and reliable materials, fabric and texture for a state-of-the-art space of comfort, peace and bliss.

Every project that comes to our operations desk, is an opportunity to transform ordinary occupancy space to an elegant haven. Our process starts from engaging with a client to know and understand their perspective of what they want, we give our due recommendations, reach an agreement and then begin on the commencement of the project through the following process;



Concept development and planning

Depending on the client's project and their desired design, we plan the space requirements, colour combination, essential items and materials that shall be needed, plan the layout in full detail and list our different suppliers for the design resources.





Visualisation

We sit down as a team and envision the full aesthetic of the client's space and environment in full detail, get guidance from some of our counter-parts and experts. After that process, we design a prototype of the final project to present to the client for review and feedback.





Material and finishing selection

Based on what the space is going to be used for, we consider balancing aesthetics with functionality considering the color theme, texture, durability and choose to use whether modern or traditional ones, light effects appearance to come up with an overall cohesive design.





Technical drawings

At this stage, we translate a design concept into a precise guide for construction, including plans, elevations, sections, and detailed specifications for everything from wall layouts to electrical wiring and finishes.





Project management
